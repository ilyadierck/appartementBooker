ABOUT

Our apartment is located in Nueva Andalucia, near Puerto Banus and a few kilometers from Marbella town. You can find many activities in the area: cozy restaurants, bars, shops, markets. In short: something for everyone.

You can find our apartment in the beautiful residence 'Albatros', with a beautifully maintained garden and a beautiful swimming pool on top of the roof where you can enjoy a breathtaking view. In our apartment you can enjoy everything that Spain is all about: sun, sea and food. The perfect place to relax!

In our apartment there is room for 6 people. It has 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. There is a fully equiped kitchen with fridge, freezer, oven, microwave and dishwasher. Our spacious living and dining room offers direct access to the terrace overlooking the garden.
